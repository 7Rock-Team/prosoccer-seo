# Editorial Calendar

_Owned by Master Strategist. Updated by Content Writer when it schedules or drafts a piece._

## How to Use

Every article idea lands here first with status `IDEA`. It moves through:

- IDEA → BRIEFED (has a written brief in `in-progress/<slug>-brief.md`)
- BRIEFED → IN PROGRESS (draft has started in `in-progress/<slug>.md`)
- IN PROGRESS → DRAFT READY (first draft complete, awaiting human writer or Mike review)
- DRAFT READY → IN REVIEW (review underway)
- IN REVIEW → PUBLISHED (live on prosoccer.com; move URL to `article-archive/_INDEX.md`)
- IN REVIEW → KILLED (rejected, reason logged in the Killed Ideas section below)

Target publish dates are aspirational until status is BRIEFED. Do not promise a date to Mike until a brief exists.
