# Article Archive Index

_Every published article has an entry here. Updated by Master Strategist after publication. This is the traceability layer that lets the Reporting agent tie organic performance back to specific articles and their contributors._

## How to Use

When an article goes live on prosoccer.com, add a row with the final URL, the primary keyword, the publish date, and the agents or humans that contributed. Revisit monthly to add the last 30-day organic session count from GA4.

## Format

| URL | Title | Primary Keyword | Published | Agents and Humans | Monthly Organic Sessions (last 30d) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| _https://prosoccer.com/..._ | _title_ | _keyword_ | _YYYY-MM-DD_ | _e.g., Content Writer + human writer + Mike_ |   |   |

## Retirement

If an article underperforms for 6 months and cannot be salvaged, log the decision here and redirect the URL. Add a row in the Retired Articles table below.
