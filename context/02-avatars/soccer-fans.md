# Avatar: Soccer Fans

_Read by: all agents. Jersey buyers, kit collectors, people who do not necessarily play but live in the sport._

## Snapshot

_Adult fan of the sport. Buys kits, scarves, training tops, boots sometimes. Follows leagues closely, often has a favorite club and a national team._

## Who They Are

- **Age range:** wide, 18 to 55.
- **Who they follow:** Premier League, Liga MX, La Liga, Champions League, USMNT, USWNT, MLS, NWSL. In LA, Mexican national team and Liga MX affinity is massive.
- **Background:** many did play growing up or play rec now.
- **Budget:** authentic kits $90 to $180 per shirt, will buy multiple per year.

## What They Actually Need

- Current season kits for the clubs and national teams they follow.
- Authenticity guarantees (official vs. replica vs. knockoff).
- Player name and number customization.
- Retro kits and commemorative pieces.
- Fan gear: scarves, beanies, training tops.

## Pain Points

- Fake jerseys sold online at real jersey prices.
- Kits that sell out in common sizes.
- Name and number customization done wrong.
- Waiting weeks for a kit when they need it for matchday.
- Shrinking on first wash.

## How They Search

- "authentic [club] jersey"
- "[club] third kit 2025 26"
- "USMNT jersey with name"
- "where to buy Mexico national team jersey"
- "difference between authentic and replica jersey"
- "[player] jersey"

## Content That Would Land With Them

- "How to spot a fake [brand] jersey"
- Kit release previews and reviews.
- Stories about the crest, the kit history, the club.
- Comparison posts: authentic vs. replica vs. stadium.

## What They Do NOT Care About

- Youth content.
- Beginner sizing guides.
- Coaching content.

## Who They Trust

- Footy Headlines.
- Classic Football Shirts.
- Reddit soccer subs.
- Their own eyes.
