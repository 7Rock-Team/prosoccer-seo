## Search Behavior Patterns in Soccer Buying

_How soccer buyers actually search. Most of this should come from keyword research once we have Ahrefs and GSC data. Put your current hypotheses here so we can test them._

Examples to validate:

- Parents search by age ("cleats for 8 year old") or size, not by brand.
- Adult players search by surface ("FG cleats", "indoor shoes", "turf cleats") and position.
- Goalkeepers search by glove cut and by pro player model ("Ulreich gloves", "Neuer gloves").
- Club coaches buy in bulk and search for training gear (cones, ladders, bibs, pugg goals).

## Constraints Unique to Soccer Retail SEO

- Licensed kits have brand-mandated content rules.
- Cleat release calendars are dictated by Nike, Adidas, Puma, New Balance, and followed by the whole industry.
- Youth product rankings are heavily parent-driven but worn by the player.
- Goalkeepers are a high-intent, low-volume niche that rewards specificity.
