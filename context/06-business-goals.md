## The One Sentence Version

_Write the single sentence that describes what ProSoccer wants from SEO over the next 12 months. Keep it concrete and measurable._

## Primary KPIs

_Mike and Tony should agree on these. Every monthly report tracks these in order._

1. Non-branded organic sessions
2. Non-branded organic revenue
3. Organic conversion rate
4. Tracked keywords in top 10
5. Tracked keywords in top 3

Track branded organic separately. Do not let branded growth cover weak non-branded performance.

_Things we watch but do not report on monthly._

- Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS)
