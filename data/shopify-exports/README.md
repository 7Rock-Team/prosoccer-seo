# Shopify Data Exports

## Sales by Sales Channel
Full-year 2024 + Q3 2025 through Q1 2026 (rolling quarters).
Shows total orders and net sales by sales channel.

**Relevant for SEO:** Online Store channel specifically. Exclude Point of Sale,
Channable (Amazon/eBay), and Draft Orders when analyzing SEO performance.

## Sales by Referrer
Q4 2025 and Q1 2026 — shows orders and revenue by traffic source and referrer name.
"search/google" rows are organic Google traffic (the core SEO baseline).

## How to use
When analyzing performance, always filter to Online Store channel first,
then segment by referrer (organic search vs other).
